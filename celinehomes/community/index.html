Community Information

A great place to live

The mid-Peninsula is a great place to live and raise a family. High-tech jobs abound, with award-winning school districts around. San Francisco and the Pacific ocean are only a short drive away, as well as two international airports. Quaint downtowns are surrounded by tree-lined streets. The weather is very mild, with temperatures half-way between San Francisco’s cold and San Jose’s heat. The mild breeze replaces any air-conditioning.
