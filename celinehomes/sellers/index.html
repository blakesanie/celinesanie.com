Sellers

People sell their homes for different reasons, and the experience is often very emotional. You can count on Celine’s personalized service to make your home selling experience as gratifying as possible.

Celine’s touch:

  • Keeping your best interest in mind at all times
  • Listening to you to choose a process best suited to your specific situation
  • In-depth market comparison to establish the best price point for your unique property
  • Extensive marketing of your property including ads, flyers, brochures, open houses, but also online marketing, tours, and more
  • Keeping you updated on similar listings in your area with automatic alerts
  • Suggesting optional improvements to boost your home value
  • Coordinating staging to add to your home’s appeal
  • Overseeing inspections
  • Guiding you to negotiate multiple offers with various contingencies and addenda
  • Coordinating the escrow process
