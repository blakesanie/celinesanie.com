About

Celine moved from France to Silicon Valley in 1994 and never looked back. She has called Menlo Park home since marrying her husband, Michael, in 1997. Their three children attend the Menlo Park public elementary schools and high school.

After many years writing/reviewing/editing technical manuals in engineering departments of high-tech companies, Celine is keeping her love for writing alive with College Essay Coaching. She became proficient with application prompts and what college admissions are looking for while guiding her two older children through the college application process.
